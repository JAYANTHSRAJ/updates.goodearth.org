Ochre Construction Progress—Second Floors Rise as All Plots Advance

April 13, 2026

The project is progressing at a strong pace overall, with several plots moving into advanced stages and eastern homes already achieving key milestones, including second floor slab completion.

Progress Overview

Wall conduit installation is a key focus right now and is actively progressing across several plots, with steady advancement on-site. The team is maintaining strong momentum and coordinating efficiently across all active units.

  • Eastern Homes: Key milestones achieved — second floor slab completion already reached in several units.
  • Western Side: Compound wall work is fully complete. Blockwork and lintel work are nearing completion across most units.
  • First Floor: Construction is well underway, with slabs completed in multiple plots across the site.
  • Second Floor: Work has begun in more advanced units, with consistent progress being maintained.
  • Wall Conduit Installation: Actively progressing across several plots with steady on-site advancement.

A Quick Word from our Projects Team

What's Next

In the coming weeks, brickwork and slab completions are scheduled for the remaining units. The team will continue to push forward on wall conduit installation and prepare for the next phase of interior work in the more advanced plots.
